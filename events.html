Christmas Markets 2021 in Berlin

The German capital Berlin has plenty to offer: from historical sites to world-class museums and a vibrant nightlife. Enjoy this private tour to explore a wide range of activities in Berlin and Potsdam, including several UNESCO World Heritage Sites. This itinerary is just a starting point as all services are 100% customisable to suit your travel wants and needs.

Amsterdam: Classic Boat Cruise

Discover the beautiful city of Amsterdam in 75-minutes whilst learning of the city's culture and rich trading history. Your cruise will take place on a covered classic canal cruise boat, away from the busy streets packed with trams, bikes, and crowds.

Pub Pod | The London Eye

Oh, and of course you'll get to take in the epic views of the London Skyline as the pod completes a rotation, with unrivalled panoramas of the likes of Big Ben and the Houses of Parliament. The venture is in partnership with Beefeater Gin, so as well as a selection of beers and wines on offer you'll get access to specially curated drinks including the new Beefeater Peach & Raspberry Gin. The new pub will open for visitors on Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays between June 4 and September 5. Opening hours will be 12pm-6pm.

EDP Lisbon Marathon

EDP Lisbon Marathon In its 7th edition, the EDP Lisbon Marathon is already considered as one of the most beautiful races in the world and acclaimed by international media such as the Forbes Magazine, the Huffington Post and American Express. Starting in Cascais and finishing at Praça do Comércio, the EDP Marathon course is 100% sea and river side, providing to the runners an unique view along the race. The EDP Lisbon Marathon is an official race of the International Athletics Federation (IAAF) calendar and it’s becoming a top international race, attracting the top elite athletes and thousands of foreign runners from all over the world.
